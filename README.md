# Government-Ready Cloud & Kubernetes Service Offerings

## 1. AI/ML-Ready GPU Platform Delivery

Deliver the design and implementation of GPU-enabled Kubernetes and OpenShift platforms engineered to support advanced analytics, artificial intelligence, and machine learning workloads. Solutions are architected for operation within secure, air-gapped, or classified environments, ensuring compliance with Federal cybersecurity mandates while enabling high-performance compute capabilities for mission-critical data processing.

## 2. Enterprise OpenShift & Kubernetes Platform Buildout

Provide full lifecycle design, deployment, and configuration of production-grade OpenShift and Kubernetes environments across bare-metal, virtualized, or hybrid infrastructures. Services include the integration of CI/CD toolchains, container registries, service mesh technologies, observability frameworks, and GPU acceleration, ensuring platforms meet government reliability, scalability, and mission assurance requirements.

## 3. Multi-Cloud Architecture & Modernization Services

Architect and implement secure, scalable hybrid and multi-cloud environments across AWS GovCloud, Azure Government, GCP, and on-premises datacenters. Modernization efforts focus on compliance with Federal policies (e.g., FedRAMP, DISA STIGs, NIST 800-53), while enabling improved system resilience, interoperability, and future-proofed cloud adoption within agency and DoD mission environments.

## 4. Hybrid Cloud Disaster Recovery & Mission Resilience Engineering

Design and implement enterprise-grade disaster recovery and continuity-of-operations (COOP) frameworks leveraging Advanced Cluster Management (ACM) and multi-site failover strategies. Solutions support hot–hot and hot–warm failover architectures, reduce Recovery Time Objectives (RTO), and maintain operational capability under contested, degraded, or disrupted conditions—aligned with government mission resilience standards.

## 5. GitOps Governance, Standardization, & Enablement

Establish standardized, policy-driven GitOps frameworks using ArgoCD or FluxCD to ensure consistent, auditable, and fully automated deployment workflows across agency environments. Services include governance policy definition, repository structure design, automation enablement, and cross-team training to support compliance, repeatability, and controlled configuration management at scale.

## 6. Advanced Cloud Security & Compliance Automation

Provide end-to-end cloud-native security engineering—including assessments, policy development, and automated enforcement—utilizing ACS, Aqua, and comparable technologies. Capabilities include continuous image scanning, CI/CD security gate enforcement, runtime threat detection, compliance automation, and reporting aligned with DISA STIGs, NIST 800-53, and agency-specific governance requirements.

## 7. Cloud Migration & Workload Modernization Services

Execute structured, repeatable migration processes from legacy datacenters to AWS GovCloud or other approved cloud environments, incorporating workload discovery, dependency analysis, modernization roadmaps, containerization strategies, and validated cutover procedures. Services ensure mission continuity, alignment with ATO requirements, and secure transition to cloud-native architectures.

## 8. Observability, Monitoring, & Operational Excellence Engineering

Implement comprehensive cloud-native observability platforms (Grafana, Kibana, OpenTelemetry) providing centralized monitoring, actionable alerting, performance analytics, and SRE-aligned operational workflows. These capabilities enhance system reliability, improve mission readiness, and reduce operational burden through automation and standardized reporting across distributed government environments.
